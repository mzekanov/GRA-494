flowers on the moon



take the time

open your mind to these words,
they are meant to guide you towards knowing
and accepting your worth and strength

behind the poems

billy chapata shares insight and advice
into the powerful world of love,
heartbreak, and what comes next.
this collection of poetry and prose
will justify heartache and inspire the
fortitude to survive and prosper.

share a poem with someone in need


darling,

you don't have to be seen to be worthy.
you are still whole without their acknowledgment.
you will still breathe without their surveillance.
you still exist.

strange paradoxes

it's the ones who view your self-love as a threat
who are actually a threat to your self-love. -ironic.

deep endeavors

true love will always try to meet you halfway.
anything else is a facade
or a well-disguised lie.

darling,

if you have to convince them of your worthiness,
they might not be worth holding on to.
poison comes in shiny bottles too.
